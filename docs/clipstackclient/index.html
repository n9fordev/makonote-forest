ClipStackClient

ClipStackClient is a free app that works with ClipStack on macOS.
It lets you view synced text on iPhone or iPad and copy what you need with one tap.

How to use Support Privacy Policy

Overview

A simple way to bring ClipStack text to iPhone and iPad.

ClipStackClient keeps the workflow simple:
open the app, find the synced text you want, and copy it to your iPhone or iPad clipboard when you need it.

  • View synced text clips from ClipStack on macOS
  • Copy a clip with one tap from the main list
  • Filter between All, Pinned, and URLs
  • Search synced history locally on the device
  • Read the latest synced history from CloudKit
  • Use encrypted sync data with a shared iCloud Keychain key
ClipStackClient does not collect clipboard history on iPhone by itself.
It is meant to be used with ClipStack already running on your Mac.
