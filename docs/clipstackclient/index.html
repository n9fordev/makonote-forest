ClipStackClient

ClipStackClient is a free app that works with ClipStack on macOS.
It lets you view synced text on iPhone or iPad and copy what you need with one tap.

How to use Support Privacy Policy

Overview

A lightweight way to bring ClipStack text to iPhone and iPad.

Open the synced item you need on iPhone or iPad only when you need it, then copy it right away.

  • Copy clips with one tap
  • Sync automatically from ClipStack on macOS
  • Keep synced text encrypted
  • Browse All, Pinned, and URLs
  • Search history on the device
ClipStackClient does not collect clipboard history on iPhone by itself.
It is meant to be used with ClipStack already running on your Mac.
