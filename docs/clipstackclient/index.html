ClipStackClient

ClipStackClient lets you save and reuse text locally on iPhone or iPad.
When paired with ClipStacks on your Mac, it can also show synced text history in the same view.

Overview

A lightweight companion that brings the ClipStacks flow to mobile.

Save text you want to keep on iPhone or iPad,
and seamlessly reuse items copied on your Mac.

  • Copy clips with one tap
  • Automatic sync from ClipStacks on macOS
  • Browse All, Pinned, and URLs
  • Search history on the device
Note: To use sync with ClipStacks on macOS,
sign in on iPhone or iPad with the same iCloud account used on your Mac.
