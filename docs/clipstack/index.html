CopyPouch

CopyPouch is a clipboard history utility for macOS.
It keeps recent text, links, and images available from a single panel.

Overview

A clipboard history tool that fits naturally into everyday work.

CopyPouch helps cut down on the back-and-forth of repeated copying and pasting between apps.

  • Pull up history quickly from a searchable panel
  • Works with text, links, and images
  • Keep important items pinned
  • Launch instantly with a hotkey
  • Preview images before reusing them
  • Check the same history on iPhone or iPad with CopyPouch Pocket
